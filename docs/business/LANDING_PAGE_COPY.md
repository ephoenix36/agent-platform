**Q: Will this work with my CRM?**
A: Yes. We integrate with Salesforce, HubSpot, Outreach, Apollo, and most major CRMs. If you use something else, we have webhooks and API access.

**Q: How long does setup take?**
A: White-glove onboarding takes 1-2 days. You upload your prospect list, we configure everything, and your first campaign goes live within 24 hours.

**Q: What if my emails go to spam?**
A: Our Email Deliverability Validator checks every email pre-send and optimizes for 90%+ inbox placement. We use provider-specific rules for Gmail, Outlook, and Apple Mail.

A: Absolutely. You can run in "approval mode" where you review every email, or "autonomous mode" where AI handles everything. Most customers start with approval mode and switch to autonomous after seeing the quality.

A: Our average customer sees 3.5% email-to-meeting conversion rate (vs. 2% industry average). That's 75% higher response rate than manual outreach.
