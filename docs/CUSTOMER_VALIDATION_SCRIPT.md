- **Budget Authority:** Can approve $5K-15K/month consulting/tools

## üó£Ô∏è **INTERVIEW SCRIPT (20 minutes)**

### Introduction (2 min)

"Thanks for taking the time. As context, I'm building an AI-powered optimization 
platform and want to make sure it actually solves real problems before investing 
months of development.

This will be conversational - I'm here to learn from your experience. Everything 
you share is confidential unless you explicitly say otherwise.

Sound good? [Wait for confirmation]"

---

### Part 1: Current State & Pain Points (7 min)

**Q1: "Walk me through your current approach to [sales optimization / climate strategy]."**

Listen for:
- Current tools/processes
- Team size and structure
- Key metrics they track
- Decision-making process

**Q2: "What's the biggest bottleneck or frustration with that approach?"**

Listen for:
- Time waste
- Manual work
- Poor results
- Lack of insights
- Tool limitations

**Q3: "What have you tried to solve this? What worked? What didn't?"**

Listen for:
- Solutions attempted
- Why they failed
- Budget spent
- What they learned

**Q4: "If you could wave a magic wand and fix one thing about [domain], what would it be?"**

Listen for:
- Dream solution
- What they value most
- What they'd pay for

---

### Part 2: Solution Validation (7 min)

**Setup:** "Let me share what we're building and get your reaction..."

**Brief Pitch (60 seconds):**
"We've built an AI system that continuously tests different strategies in 
[their domain], learns what works best for your specific situation, and 
automatically optimizes over time. 

For example, in sales, it might test 50 different email approaches, identify 
the top 3 performers, and focus your team's effort there - leading to 15-75% 
improvement in response rates.

The key difference from typical tools: instead of you manually running A/B 
tests or hiring consultants, the system runs hundreds of experiments 
automatically and continuously improves."

**Q5: "Gut reaction - does this sound valuable for your situation?"**

Listen for:
- Enthusiasm level
- Skepticism points
- Questions they ask
- Comparison to alternatives

**Q6: "What would make you confident this would actually work for you?"**

Listen for:
- Proof needed (case studies, trials, guarantees)
- Implementation concerns
- Trust factors
- Decision criteria

**Q7: "What would prevent you from using something like this, even if it worked?"**

Listen for:
- Budget constraints
- Internal politics
- Competing priorities
- Technical limitations

---

### Part 3: Pricing & Commitment (4 min)

**Q8: "If this achieved [15-75% improvement in their key metric], what would that be worth to you?"**

Listen for:
- ROI calculation
- Budget ranges
- Value perception

**Q9: "We're pricing the platform at $2,500-$15,000 per month depending on scale. Reaction?"**

Listen for:
- Too high/low/just right
- What would justify the price
- Comparison to alternatives

**Q10: "We're looking for 3-5 beta customers to use the platform at 50% off for 3 months in exchange for feedback. Would you be interested?"**

Listen for:
- Level of interest
- Conditions/requirements
- Timeline
- Decision process

---

### Closing (2 min)

"This has been incredibly valuable. A few final things:

1. **May I follow up** with you as we build this out?
2. **Is there anyone else** you'd recommend I talk to?
3. **What else** should I have asked that I didn't?

I'll send you:
- Summary of what I learned across all interviews
- Early access when we launch
- [Specific value promised earlier]

Best way to stay in touch? [Get email/LinkedIn]

Thank you again!"
